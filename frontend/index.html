MD5 & SHA Checksum Utility Pro 3.0

MD5 & SHA Checksum Utility Pro 3.0 allows you to quickly generate hashes including CRC32, CRC64, MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, SHA-384, SHA-512, SHA3-256, SHA3-512, BLAKE2B, BLAKE2S, and BLAKE3 for your files and folders. It also lets you verify file and folder integrity with ease. New in version 3.0: compare entire folders, command-line interface (CLI) support, user-selectable hash case (upper/lower), and enhanced portable mode. All hashing is performed locally on your device to ensure data privacy and security.
